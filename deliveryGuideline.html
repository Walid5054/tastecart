Delivery Guidelines

📋 General Rules

  • Always wear your TasteCart uniform during deliveries.
  • Be polite and professional with customers and restaurants.
  • Keep your delivery bag clean and secure.
  • Never deliver food tampered or opened.

🚦 Pickup Guidelines

  • Check the order ID before picking up food.
  • Verify food packaging and seal before leaving the restaurant.
  • If there's a delay at the restaurant, inform the customer using the app.

🛵 Delivery Guidelines

  • Use maps to reach destinations safely and quickly.
  • Call customers if you can't find the exact location.
  • Never pressure customers for tips.
  • Mark orders delivered only after handing them over.

⚠️ Safety & Emergency

  • In case of accidents, inform TasteCart support immediately.
  • Report any suspicious behavior or fraudulent orders.
  • Keep emergency contacts updated in your profile.
